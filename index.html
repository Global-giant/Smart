SHE CODES AFRICA

She Codes Africa (SCA) is a non-profit organization focused on celebrating and empowering young Girls and Women in Technology across Africa.

      • She Codes Africa has grown active membership from 0 to 17,000+ within 12 months in 20+ countries.
        She Codes Africa holds a yearly summit that enables young african girls to network with other people who are in the same field as them or who are professionals in the global tech world.
        She Codes Africa give laptop scholarships every year to passionate technology driven ladies who can not afford a laptop.
        Their goal is to build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, mentorship and visibility amongst all level career roles in Technology.
